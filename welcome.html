Welcome to Tomato Leaf Disease Prediction

Tomato plants are susceptible to various diseases that can affect their growth and yield.

Use this web application to upload images of tomato leaves and predict diseases for early detection and management.

The following are common diseases that can affect tomato plants:

  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Leaf Mold
  • Bacterial Spot
